Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy

This app is a simple tool for distributing eSIM codes and sharing basic manual setup instructions. It does not include any form of user interaction, messaging, or dynamic content exchange.

🚫 No User-Generated Content or Social Features

  • Users cannot upload, share, or comment on content within the app.
  • There are no social features, chats, profiles, or media sharing options.
  • The only optional input is an eSIM code and a recipient email for donation purposes.

🔐 Privacy & Safety Commitment

Since no personal data or multimedia is collected or stored by the app, the risk of misuse or CSAE-related violations is effectively eliminated.

The app is not targeted at children and includes no content designed for minors.
