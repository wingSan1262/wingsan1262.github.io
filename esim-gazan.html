Privacy Policy

This app is designed to help users in Gaza securely redeem donated eSIM data packages. We value your privacy and only collect minimal data required for functionality and fairness.

1. Personal Information

We collect only your name and email when you redeem a gift. This information is stored securely in Firebase Firestore and used solely to:

  • Prevent abuse (limit redemption to once every 4 hours)
  • Show your redeemed gift history

We do not share or sell your personal information.

2. Location Usage

The app checks your location to ensure that gift redemptions are made only from Gaza. Your exact location is not stored or shared.

3. QR Scanning

When you scan a QR code, the app reads the embedded eSIM activation string for installation guidance. This data is not uploaded or tracked.

4. Firebase Services

We use Firebase Firestore to store gift and user information, and Firebase Crashlytics to improve stability. Crash reports may include technical info (device model, OS version) but never personal identity.

5. Internet Permissions

Internet access is used only to load and submit data to Firebase and fetch redemption info.

6. No Ads or Analytics

This app does not serve ads or track behavior with analytics tools.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the app maintainer or sponsoring organization.
