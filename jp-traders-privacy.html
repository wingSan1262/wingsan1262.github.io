Privacy Policy

This app is designed to assist verified retail partners in placing order requests based on our public distribution stock list.

1. No Personal Information Collected

We do not collect, store, or share any personal user information through this app. All registration and onboarding processes are handled externally by our team.

2. Permissions Used

The app uses only the permissions necessary to function:

  • Internet Access – to display available products and pricing updates.
  • Notification Access – to notify users about new products, price changes, and stock updates.

No personal data is accessed or transmitted through these permissions.

3. Crash Reporting

We use Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix app crashes or bugs. Crash reports may include non-personal technical data such as device model, OS version, and error logs. This information is used solely to improve app stability and is not linked to personal identity or behavior.

4. Public Content Only

All product information displayed in the app — such as product names, salt compositions, and prices — is public and not tied to individual user data.

5. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact your assigned admin or reach out via our official WhatsApp channel.
